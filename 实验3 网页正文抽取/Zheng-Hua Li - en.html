Introduction

I am currently an associate professor (since Jul. 2016) and a master student supervisor (since Dec. 2017) in Human Language Technology Research Center (HLT) at School of Computer Science and Technology at Soochow University. I obtained my Bachelor, Master, and PhD degree from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2006, 2008, and 2013, respectively, and joined Soochow Univeristy afterwards.

My recent interests include:

  • Word segmentation (WS): multi-grained WS; WS on web texts
  • Syntactic parsing: exploiting different kinds of semantic knowledge
  • Semantic parsing: shallow semantic parsing (semenatic role labeling, SRL), deep semantic parsing (such as AMR, Abstract Meaning Representation)
  • Building a data annotation platform for NLP tasks: support online many-user annotation; scientific quality control; reasonable workflow control
